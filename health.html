Gene

Bio-Med // Zurich

Allergy-Stop is the first gene-editing therapy approved for pediatric use. By re-coding the immune system to ignore specific proteins like peanuts and pollen, the Zurich Institute has effectively cured 98% of chronic allergies in trial participants. It is the greatest medical leap since the discovery of antibiotics.

The Precision Age

Medical treatments are no longer one-size-fits-all. We have entered the era of "N-of-1" medicine, where therapies are synthesized specifically for your unique DNA profile. This approach is turning once-terminal illnesses into manageable conditions, radically extending the healthy human lifespan.
