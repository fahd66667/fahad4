Viewpoint // London

As AI masters the logical world, the "Human Premium" has arrived. We argue that emotional intelligence and empathy are now the most valuable skills in the global market. Machines can compute, but they cannot care. That is our new superpower, and it is the only thing that cannot be automated.

The Soul in the Machine

We must resist the urge to compete with AI on its terms. Instead, we should double down on the qualities that make us uniquely human: our capacity for nuance, our moral compass, and our ability to find meaning in suffering. In the next decade, the most successful people will not be the smartest, but the most deeply connected.
