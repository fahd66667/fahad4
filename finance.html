Ledger

Economics // Singapore

Fiat currency has entered its final decade. With Southeast Asia leading the charge into sovereign blockchain tokens, the global banking system is decentralizing. This shift has eliminated transaction fees for 2 billion people but has sparked a massive debate over government surveillance through public ledgers and the loss of financial anonymity.

Decentralized Sovereignty

As the "Big Five" central banks prepare to launch their own digital currencies, the battle for the future of value is intensifying. We are witnessing the rise of programmable money—capital that can only be spent on specific goods or that expires if not used, creating a radical new toolkit for economic engineering.
