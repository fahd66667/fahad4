Transit // Paris-Zurich

The Magnet-Link rail network has connected Paris to Berlin in just 88 minutes. These zero-emission trains travel inside vacuum tubes at 600km/h, making regional air travel obsolete for most of Europe. This marks a new era of ultra-fast, sustainable transit that is effectively shrinking the continent.

The Death of the Airport

As the network expands into Asia and North America, the concept of a "long-haul" flight is becoming a relic of the past. Travel is becoming seamless—no security lines, no delays, just a continuous, high-speed flow of people and goods across borders that are increasingly becoming purely digital.
